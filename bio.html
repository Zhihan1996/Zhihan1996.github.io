Bio

Previously I was a research assistant at Department of Mathematics of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Yuan Yao and Prof. Jian-Feng Cai. I worked towards my master research under the supervision of Emilie Chouzenoux and Jean-Christophe Pesquet at CentraleSupélec, Université Paris-Saclay, France. I completed my previous degrees at HKUST and The University of Hong Kong (HKU) respectively. Please refer to my résumé for further details.
