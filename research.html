Research Interests

My major research interests are in natural language processing, deep learning and deep reinforcement learning. In particular, I am interested in

  • Dialogue system(including task-oriented and chitchat)

  • Natural language generation

  • Natural language understanding


I am also working on the development and maintenance of a private computer cluster consists of 8 nodes. I deploy a distributed training framework that communicating with InfiniBand on this cluster to enable effecient distributed computation.
